Frequently Asked Questions

Will my utility bill increase?+
No. Sabey covers all incremental costs for dedicated infrastructure. Community power rates remain unchanged.
How much water will the facility use?+
The Indianapolis project uses a closed-loop cooling system that requires virtually no water during normal operation.
What about noise or traffic?
Noise and traffic levels are significantly below typical industrial uses. Independent studies show 80% fewer trips than previously approved plans.
How does this benefit our community?
The project brings major investment, better roads, and job opportunities — strengthening Decatur Township for the long term.
