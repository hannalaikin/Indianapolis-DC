What’s inside a data center?

Think of it as reliable infrastructure that keeps hospitals, schools, businesses, and public agencies online — with no crowds or retail activity, just secure rooms with computers and advanced cooling.

Our Story

Who is Sabey Data Centers?

For over 25 years, Sabey has designed, built, and operated highly efficient data centers across the U.S. With six active campuses and over 3.5M square feet delivered, Sabey is known for reliability, environmental stewardship, and long-term community partnerships.

Why Indiana — and why Decatur Township?

Indiana’s stable business climate, strong utility partnerships, and skilled workforce make it a natural home for technology infrastructure. Decatur Township offers logistics access and community leadership to build a well-buffered campus that fits its surroundings.
